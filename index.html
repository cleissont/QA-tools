The solution

QA-tools is the solution that automates areas for software quality and assurance. Where it is possible to manage, easily track and report all quality indicators in real time, Ensure compliance and focus on customer needs and satisfaction. Processes throughout the organization.

Quality

Testing from the beginning of development is advised not only by agile methodologies, but as can be seen in Myers, indicated for quality assurance, since the tests are performed from the beginning to the end of the development process.

Key benefits of the solution

Increased security in operations involving quality management. Greater efficiency in the identification and mapping of processes, implementation and monitoring of policies. Elimination of delays and inefficiencies that usually occur in manual activities. Increased productivity throughout the quality management system. Accuracy in identifying regulatory requirements that affect the performance of the quality management system.
